The why behind Know Wallet

A message from our founder & CEO

It's disheartening to hear about entrepreneurs who have lost their hard-earned funds as a

result of security breaches. Unfortunately, this was the case with my friend who stored

his startup funds in a cryptocurrency wallet. Despite his best efforts to maintain security, an

unfortunate incident occurred as a result of an employee's mistake, compromising the wallet

and leading to the theft of his startup funds.

 

Upon hearing about his situation, I decided to take action and create Know Wallet - a

revolutionary platform designed to help people track their cryptocurrency wallets and monitor

their digital assets. With Know Wallet, you can easily track multiple wallets and receive instant

notifications if any unauthorized transactions occur, allowing you to take prompt action to

protect your investments.

 

We understand the risks that come with holding cryptocurrency, but we also believe in its

potential for entrepreneurs. That's why Know Wallet is dedicated to providing an extra layer

of security to safeguard your digital assets. Our platform is designed to give you peace of

mind and the confidence to invest in cryptocurrency without worrying about the security

of your funds.

 

We're confident that you'll love Know Wallet. Give it a try and see how it can help you safeguard

your digital assets. If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out to us

at deveshnair@know-wallet.com

 

Sincerely,

Devesh Nair

CEO, Know Wallet
